Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2025

1. Application Overview

N8N Niezaam is a personal Gmail OAuth application that integrates with Claude Desktop MCP server for personal email management and AI-assisted productivity.

2. Information We Access

This application accesses:

  • Your Gmail messages and metadata
  • Your Google account email address
  • Your basic profile information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Reading, composing, sending, and organizing your emails
  • Providing AI-assisted email management through Claude Desktop
  • Automating personal productivity workflows

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your personal information
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Store your emails on external servers

5. Data Security

Your data is processed locally through Claude Desktop MCP server. OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local machine and are not transmitted to external servers.

6. Data Retention

This application does not retain your email data. All processing is done in real-time through the Claude Desktop interface.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke access to this application at any time through your Google Account settings.
